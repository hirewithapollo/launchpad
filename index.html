Hey, let's talk about hiring.

Hiring is really hard.

If you're lucky and know someone awesome, then it's easy. Otherwise you have to contend with numerous job boards, endless emails, and an ever-present sense of exhaustion.

We've been there.

We're a couple of people who've felt that feeling you're feeling right now. We've been overwhelmed by the number of inbound emails. We've lost track of which sourcing tool that great candidate was from. We've wished for better insights into our hiring process.

Apollo is what we wished existed.

You shouldn't have to spend so much time and energy hiring folks. There should be software that takes care of most of it for you, so you can go back to doing what you do best!

We're building Apollo for you. We're talking to real people about their experiences. We're learning what other people are doing today, what's great about it and what sucks. Building a business is really hard, we're hoping to make it just a little bit easier.

We don't want to create just another hiring tool. It's not ready yet, but it will be soon.

- The Apollo Team
